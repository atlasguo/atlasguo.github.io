LEGO Mapping

A special collection of my LEGO-style maps

Below is the collection of my recent explorations in LEGO / LEGO-Style mapping. Before 2024, I didn’t even consider myself a LEGO fan. But by chance, I ended up starting my very first LEGO mapping project—for no reason other than curiosity. And then, this collection keeps going...
