Wisconsin Map on Solar Printed Cotton Bandana

Made with local flowers and fronds during a bike-and-craft activity

I made this solar printed Wisconsin map on bandana during a bike-and-craft activity organized by Cap City Cyclist, a Madison's cycling advocacy group.

Due to the lack of experience, the plants I collected are not well aligned as expected under the windy condition, but it was really fun. The bandana looks like this before being put under the sunshine.
