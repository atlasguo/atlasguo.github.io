Ancient Chinese Style Map Design

Basemap design with ancient Chinese fonts, icons, and color scheme based on Mapbox Studio

This Mapbox web map is customized in style of ancient Chinese map, mostly considering the color style of antique map and traditional Chinese painting, the font selection in ancient Chinese calligraphy style, and ancient style iconic point symbols. Due to the limited place names in native language, this web map is designed for the extent of China only, while other regions might encounter font displaying issue.
