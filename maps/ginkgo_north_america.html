Ginkgo Painting of North America

Painted with topographic color scheme on a fan-shape ginkgo leave under conic projection

After picking up some ginkgo leaves, I made a conic projection map (Albers conic), which naturally has a fan shape as these leaves. Then I draw this topographic map of North America.
