Geog 370 - Introduction to Cartography

UW-Madison Summer 2024 Online Course (4-Credit)

This is the Summer 2024 offering of UW-Madison Geog 370 - Introduction to Cartography. The instructor is Atlas (Chenxiao) Guo (he/him), a Ph.D. student at the Department of Geography, UW-Madison. Students could contact Atlas via Slack (access to enrolled students only), email (atlas.guo@wisc.edu), or during Office Hour (Zoom).

The Syllabus (Google Docs) and Schedule (Google Sheets) will be up-to-date, while the specifics are subject to change with announcement in Slack. All deliverables are due 11:59 PM CDT, with late deduction of 10% per day (not acceptable after a week). As a 4-credit course in 12 weeks, this course expects up to 15 hours of work per week. Please use the links on Canvas (access to enrolled students only) to navigate the materials, and more links will be enabled as the course progresses.

Happy Mapping!
